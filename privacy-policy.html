1 · Introduction

Rehearse Management ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") is developed and operated by Everett Randolph Anton, an individual developer based in Colorado, United States.

This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect when you use the App, how we use and protect that information, and the choices you have. By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

The App is an invitation-only scheduling and communication tool for performing arts productions. It is not a public consumer service and is not directed at children.

2 · Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account credentials — Your email address and password. Passwords are managed exclusively by Firebase Authentication (Google LLC) using industry-standard hashing; we never store or have access to your plain-text password.
  • Profile information — Your name and your role within a production (e.g., actor, stage manager, director, technician).
  • Conflict and availability submissions — Dates on which you are unavailable or partially available, the type of conflict (all-day, arriving late, or leaving early), an associated time, and any optional explanatory notes you choose to include.
  • Announcements and messages — Content you compose and send to other production members through the App's communications features.

2.2 Information Created by Production Managers

Authorized production managers may create or record the following information about you in the App:

  • Your membership in a production and your assigned role(s)
  • Whether you are called to specific rehearsals or set builds
  • Your attendance status at rehearsals (present, late, excused, or absent)
  • Conflict entries submitted on your behalf

2.3 Information Collected Automatically

  • Push notification token — If you enable push notifications, your device generates a unique token that is stored and used solely to deliver notifications from the App to your device.
  • Firebase usage data — Google Firebase may collect basic technical information (such as device type, operating system version, and crash reports) as part of its standard infrastructure. We do not use Firebase Analytics or any advertising SDKs.

3 · How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Authenticate your identity and provide you access to the App
  • Display production schedules, rehearsal calls, and set build information to authorized members
  • Present your submitted conflicts to production managers for review and approval
  • Record and display rehearsal attendance
  • Send push notifications about rehearsal reminders, schedule changes, attendance alerts, and announcements
  • Enable communications between production managers and members
  • Support the live show management features of the App
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the App

We do not use your information for advertising, do not build advertising profiles, and do not sell your data to any third party.

4 · How We Share Your Information

4.1 Within Your Production

Your name, role, schedule, and conflict information is visible to other authorized members of your production as required to fulfill the App's scheduling and communication functions. Managers have access to all member data within their production. Members can view production-wide schedules and may see which other members are called to rehearsals.

4.2 Service Providers

We share data with the following third-party service providers who process data on our behalf:

  • Google Firebase (Google LLC) — We use Firebase Authentication for secure login and Firebase Firestore (a cloud database) to store all production, scheduling, and member data. Google processes this data under its standard data processing agreements. See Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Expo (Expo Inc.) — We use Expo's push notification infrastructure to deliver notifications to your device. Expo processes your device token for this purpose only. See Expo's Privacy Policy at expo.dev/privacy.

We do not share your information with any other third parties except as required by law.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, regulation, court order, or governmental authority, or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

5 · Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide the App's services. If your access is revoked by a production manager, your account data may be retained in our systems unless you separately request deletion.

To request deletion of your personal data, contact us at privacy@everettanton.com. We will process your request within 30 days, subject to any legal obligations to retain certain information.

6 · Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encrypted data transmission (TLS), Firebase's built-in authentication security, and Firestore security rules that restrict data access to authorized users only.

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7 · Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App requires an invitation from an authorized production manager to access, and is intended for use by adults and older teenagers in professional or semi-professional performing arts contexts.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information without appropriate consent, please contact us at privacy@everettanton.com and we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

8 · Your Privacy Rights

8.1 General Rights

Regardless of where you are located, you may:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw your consent to push notifications at any time through your device's notification settings

8.2 Colorado Residents

If you are a Colorado resident, you may have additional rights under the Colorado Privacy Act (C.R.S. § 6-1-1301 et seq.), including the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Delete personal data provided by or obtained about you
  • Opt out of the sale of personal data (note: we do not sell personal data)
  • Obtain a portable copy of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, submit a request to privacy@everettanton.com. We will respond within 45 days, with a possible 45-day extension when reasonably necessary.

8.3 California Residents

If you are a California resident, you may have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), including rights to know, delete, and opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information. We do not sell or share personal information as defined under California law.

To submit a privacy request, contact us at privacy@everettanton.com.
