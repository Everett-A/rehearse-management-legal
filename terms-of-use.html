2 · Description of Service

Rehearse Management is a scheduling, attendance, conflict management, and communication tool designed for use by performing arts organizations. The App enables production managers to create and manage rehearsal and set build schedules, track member attendance, review and approve member availability conflicts, manage live show states, and communicate with cast and crew. Members can view their schedule, submit availability conflicts, and receive communications within their assigned production(s).

The App is intended for use exclusively by individuals who have been granted access by an authorized production manager. It is not available for self-registration by the general public.
